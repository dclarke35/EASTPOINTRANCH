The Details

When

Sunday, June 30, 2023

  • Ceremony: 4:00 pm
  • Cocktails: 4:30 pm
  • Reception: 6:00 pm
  • Dinner: 6:30 pm

Where

67413 AB-Highway 633

Parkland County, AB

When you get there you will see two driveways on the southside of highway 633. We will be celebrating in the driveway to the west with the address above.

This is my aunt and uncle's home. I (Dani) spent a great deal of time on this acreage and it means a lot to not only myself but a lot of my cousin that grew up loving our time here. It is my aunt's generational property and she has spent only about 4 short years away from it all her life.

What

  • Dress: "Smart Casual"

    This just means that you are welcome to wear your jeans and boots as long as you dress it up with a nice dress shirt.

  • Ceremony

    Unlike this webpage the ceremony will be kept short and informal. 20 to 30 minutes.

  • Cocktails, Games & Snacks

    We will be serving snacks after the ceremony as well as beverages. While everything gets set for dinner you will be encouraged to try your hand at a yard game or two to pass the time.

  • Dinner: Texas Style Barbeque

    We will be serving an assortment of barbequed meats including brisket and pulled pork with all the fixings you come to excpect with those. Also, perogies! Because this is Alberta after all.

  • Atmosphere: Intimate · Family Friendly · Rustic · Country

    We are having close family and friends join us for the day. No more than 80 people, including kids, will be attending. And yes, your kids are more than welcome! So much of why we are close with our people are because of our kiddos. You may want to find childcare for the later festivities as we do plan to dance and celebrate well into the night.

  • Colors: Dusty Pinks and Burgundy

    No need to be worried if your preffered outfit is in this range! No one will be offended if you are in a similar color to the bridal party, group pictures will look all the more color coordinated.
